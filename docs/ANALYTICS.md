# Analytics and Reporting

- Campaign dashboard: spend to date, CPM effective, views, participants, top performers
- Participant dashboard: earnings breakdown, metrics over time, submission statuses
- Platform KPIs: GMV, payouts, take rate, fraud rate, approval rates
- Data warehouse (later): nightly exports to S3 + DuckDB/BigQuery
- Tracking: server-side events with event schema; privacy-safe aggregation
